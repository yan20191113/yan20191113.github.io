Short Bio

Dr. Yan Zhao is a Professor with University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UEST). She received the Ph.D. degree of Computer Science from Soochow University (advised by Prof. Kai Zheng), in 2020. She received the Master degree of Geographic Information System from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, in 2015. Her research interests include spatial database, trajectory computing, data mining, machine learning and deep learning.
